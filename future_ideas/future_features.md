# Future Ideas and Features

This document serves as a repository for future features and ideas that are outside the scope of the current Phase MVPs, but provide strong potential for future upselling and product expansion.

## 1. Interactive AI Call Agent Demo
**Context:** For the Technical Upsell Module within the Merchant Dashboard.
**Idea:** Instead of just text describing the AI Call Agent services, we can build a stylized, interactive chat or voice widget directly on the dashboard. 
- Merchants can click a "Test the AI Agent" button.
- It opens a simulated interface where they can pretend to be a customer asking about store hours or booking an appointment.
- This creates an immediate "aha!" moment, proving the technology's value before they even get on a sales call with you.

## 2. Gamified Milestone Progress Bars
**Context:** For the Merchant Dashboard auto-generated impact reports.
**Idea:** Introduce progress bars visually driving the merchant to their next milestone.
- "You are 75 votes away from unlocking the GTA Fan Favorite Badge!"
- Psychologically incentivizes the merchant to push their QR codes harder and share their link more aggressively on social media.
